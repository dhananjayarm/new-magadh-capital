Vipul Prasad, CEO/Designated Partner –

With a wealth of experience spanning over several domains, Vipul has been at the helm of Magadh Capital PMS as its fund manager since August 2021. Prior to this role, he served as the fund manager for Magadh Capital's long-only equity fund (Alternative Investment Fund, Category III) for approximately three years. Vipul has more than twenty years’ experience in Indian equity markets and has experienced several bull and bear cycles.

Vipul's started his equity markets journey as an equity research analyst with stints at esteemed institutions such as Morgan Stanley, ABN Amro, and Motilal Oswal. Notably, he held the position of Executive Director at Morgan Stanley's India equity research team before venturing into establishing Magadh Capital.

Before delving into the intricacies of the financial world, Vipul gained invaluable experience in diverse fields, including product management at BASF India and roles in maintenance, planning, and operations at SAIL. He holds an MBA (PGDM, Finance) from IIM Calcutta and a B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering from IIT BHU, Varanasi.

Beyond his professional endeavors, Vipul is an avid reader with a penchant for non-fiction literature, and is particularly interested in topics such as history, psychology, geopolitics, and economics. His insights are not confined to the pages of books; he also shares his perspectives through op-ed articles in various business newspapers and makes occasional appearances on business news TV channels.

In essence, Vipul Prasad provides leadership and expertise in the world of investment management to Magadh Capital PMS. He brings a blend of experience, education, and intellectual curiosity to his role as the head of Magadh Capital PMS.

With a distinguished career spanning over two and half decades, Purnendra, fondly known as PK, has served as a CXO across diverse sectors including Financial Services, Family Office, EdTech, Technology, and EPC, for esteemed Indian, US, and European MNCs.

Throughout his professional journey, PK has showcased his entrepreneurial and intrapreneurial acumen, spearheading the incubation, scaling, and turnaround of businesses. Notably, he has played a pivotal role in setting up a family office and has led many M&A initiatives on a global scale. PK is a well-respected figure in academia, serving as a visiting faculty at several leading B-Schools. He imparts knowledge on topics such as Innovation and Entrepreneurship, and Corporate Strategy, showcasing his dedication to student development and societal empowerment through technology-led education and skill development. He is a recipient of the IIM Calcutta Gold Medal and Roll of Honour, as well as the Mobar Gold Medal, underscoring his academic excellence and leadership prowess.

Overall Purnendra Kishore brings a wealth of experience, leadership, expertise, and academic prowess to our team at Magadh Capital, driving rigour and excellence in the realm of PMS.
